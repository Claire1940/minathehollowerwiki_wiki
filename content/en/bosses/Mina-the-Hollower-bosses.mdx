One of the first significant encounters players might face is the formidable Gator Boss. This early-game mini-boss has been noted by players for its challenging nature, especially if one relies solely on brute force rather than strategic play.

| Attack Name | Description | Counter Strategy |
|:------------|:------------|:-----------------|
| **Charge Attack** | Charges directly at Mina, covering significant ground. | Burrow underground or dodge to the side. Timing is critical as the burrow animation has a slight delay. |
| **Bite Combo** | A series of quick, close-range bites. | Keep distance. After the combo, there's a brief opening for attacks. |
| **Tail Swipe** | A wide-arc swipe that can hit if Mina is too close to its rear. | Stay in front or at a safe distance. Can be dodged by moving under the boss. |
| **Environmental Slam** | May slam its body, causing minor tremors or falling debris. | Observe visual cues and move to safe zones. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Dagger Wielders Beware">
Players using the twin daggers might find the Gator Boss particularly challenging. The daggers' short range necessitates surgical precision and excellent spacing, making it harder to get hits in without taking damage compared to longer-range weapons or subweapons. Consider using subweapons for added reach.
</Callout>

### Recommended Strategy for Gator Boss

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Observe and Learn">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Spacing">
    Maintain a medium distance. Get in one or two hits with your whip (Nightstar) or chosen weapon, then immediately prepare to evade. Don't overcommit to attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Burrow for Evasion">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Exploit Openings">
