<Section icon={Map} title="Phase Patterns and Attack Cues">

Understanding the Kraken’s telegraphs is the key to victory. The boss consistently signals its attacks before they execute, allowing you to reposition effectively.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Claw Strikes">
    - Claws tilt upward and shake before striking.
    - Order varies throughout the fight.
    - Always watch the movement of the claws.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tentacle Sweeps">
    - Tentacles shake briefly before sweeping.
    - They move directly toward your current position.
    - Some sweeps occur immediately upon landing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Special Roar">
    - Kraken heaves both claws and roars.
    - Indicates a high-speed sequence of attacks.
    - Prioritize burrowing and jumping over attacking.
  </Card>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch for the Roar">
When the Kraken roars, stop attacking immediately. This sequence is fast and unforgiving; your only goal during this phase is to maintain mobility through burrowing and jumping.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combat Strategy">

Follow these steps to optimize your damage output while minimizing the risk of taking unnecessary hits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Pre-fight Damage">
    After the first cutscene, ignore the tentacle grabbing the passenger. Focus on hitting the tentacles that appear on the deck immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Target the Left Claw">
    When the camera shifts, the Kraken will wait before its first attack. Strike the left claw twice to shave off HP before the battle officially begins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Defensive Spacing">
    Stick to the left side of the arena. Move vertically (up or down) to dodge incoming sweeps rather than trying to outrun them horizontally.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Limit Attack Windows">
    Do not overstay your welcome. Hit the tentacles only twice before moving away to reset your position.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Attack Phase | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Tentacle Sweep** | Move up or down to create distance. |
| **Standard Claw Attack** | Strike twice then retreat. |
| **Roar Sequence** | Focus on defense, jump, and burrow. |
