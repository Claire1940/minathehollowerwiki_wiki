<Section icon={Map} title="Nether Kraken Attack Patterns and Evasion">
Once the main fight begins, the camera will fixate on the right side of the ship. Your primary goal is to avoid the Kraken's various tentacle and claw attacks while landing hits on its vulnerable appendages.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Main Arena Positioning">
    Stick to the left side of the boss arena. This provides more reaction time to anticipate and dodge incoming attacks from the right.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Dodging Claw Attacks">
    The Kraken always raises its claws before attacking. They will tilt upward and shake briefly, indicating which claw is about to strike. Move up or down to get out of the attack path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Handling Sweeping Tentacles">
    Tentacles on the deck will usually shake briefly before sweeping towards Mina. Move away or burrow. Be aware that sometimes they sweep immediately after landing without a shake.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Responding to Special Fast Attacks">
    If the Kraken heaves both claws and "roars," prepare for a rapid sequence of claw attacks that don't leave tentacles on the deck. During this phase, focus entirely on burrowing and jumping to evade until it returns to its standard attack pattern.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Burrowing Mechanics">
Although the game doesn't explicitly teach it yet, you can use Mina's burrow ability by holding the jump button after landing. This is incredibly effective for sliding under incoming claw attacks and providing invulnerability frames.
</Callout>

This powerful item enhances your damage output, but it comes with a trade-off: you will also receive increased damage from enemies.
