To advance through *Mina the Hollower*, you'll need to activate a specific number of mirrors to power a spark generator in the Astral Orrery. This intricate system requires strategic mirror activation to unlock new areas.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Mirror">
    Find the mirror in the **Grand Hall of Radiant Manor Foyer**. This is the first step towards accessing the Astral Orrery from this region.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Mirror World">
    Unlike typical interactions, you must **repeatedly run into the mirror** until you are transported to the Astral Orrery. Do not simply press the interaction button.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate the Mechanism">
    Once inside the Astral Orrery, locate and activate the **gold mechanism**. This action unlocks the associated room and allows you to freely explore Mirror's End, returning through other activated mirrors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Additional Mirrors">
    To fully progress, you'll need to activate a minimum of **three mirrors** in total across Tenebrous Isle. Each activated mirror unlocks its corresponding colored path in Mirror's End.
  </Step>
</Steps>
