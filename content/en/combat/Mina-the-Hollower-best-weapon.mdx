### The Night Star (The Reliable Choice)
As Mina’s signature weapon, the Night Star functions similarly to the iconic Belmont whip. It provides a safe buffer between you and the island's horrific creatures. Its primary strength lies in its ability to interact with the environment—lighting candles, triggering switches, and even manipulating your own sidearms in mid-air.

### Blaststrike Maul (The Speedrunner’s Pick)
The Blaststrike Maul is a high-risk, high-reward tool. While its base attack speed is slow, holding the attack button allows Mina to charge up a massive explosive strike. While charging, you can move to perform a roll that grants brief invulnerability. Experienced players use this roll to "phase" through enemy hitboxes before delivering a crushing blow.

<Callout type="warning" title="Mobility Trade-off">
The Maul significantly reduces your mobility if you aren't constantly utilizing the charge-roll. If you find yourself getting hit frequently, switch back to the Night Star until you master the roll timing.
</Callout>

### Building Your Loadout
1. **The Tank**: Equip Weighted Plating and focus Bone Up points into Health and Defense. Use the Blaststrike Maul to trade hits effectively.
2. **The Assassin**: Equip Sharpened Flint and Lung Capacity. Use the Dark Star and Daggers for high-mobility, high-burst damage.
3. **The Saboteur**: Focus on Poison Bombs and the Battery Buster. Use trinkets that extend status effect durations to melt boss health bars.

<Callout type="warning" title="Death Penalties">
When you die, your accumulated bones are dropped at the site of your death. If you die again before retrieving them, they are lost forever. Always spend your bones at a checkpoint before entering a boss arena.
