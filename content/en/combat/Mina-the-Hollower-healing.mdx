Mina the Hollower presents a challenging yet rewarding healing mechanic that blends elements from classic Zelda and Souls-like games. The core of Mina the Hollower healing revolves around "Plasma." When you take damage, a portion of your health bar turns yellow, representing Plasma. To convert this Plasma back into red health, you must attack enemies. Each successful hit you land fills the yellow section, and once enough Plasma is accumulated, you can use a Plasma Vial to restore a significant chunk of your health. This offensive-oriented healing system ensures that even when low on health, you're compelled to press the attack, adding a layer of intensity to every encounter.

<Callout type="tip" title="Plasma Vial Management">
Always keep an eye on your Plasma gauge. Actively attacking enemies not only deals damage but also charges your healing vials, making proactive offense your best defense.
</Callout>
