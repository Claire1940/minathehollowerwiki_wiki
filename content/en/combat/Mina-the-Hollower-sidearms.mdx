| Sidearm Name | Attack Pattern | Key Advantage | Best Used Against |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Synergy: Combining Sidearms with the Night Star">

One of the most innovative aspects of **Mina the Hollower sidearms** is how they interact with Mina’s primary whip, the Night Star. Rather than being two separate systems, the whip and the sidearms are designed to be used in tandem. This synergy allows for "cheeky double hits" and environmental triggers that can turn the tide of a difficult battle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Ranged Pressure">
    Start the encounter by throwing a sidearm like the Gyro Dagger or Spear. This forces the enemy to react and often opens up their guard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Whip Interaction">
    As the sidearm performs its function (like the Dagger returning), use the Night Star to strike the projectile. This can redirect the weapon or trigger an explosion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Burrow Follow-up">
    While the sidearm is dealing damage, use the burrow mechanic to dive underground. This repositioning allows you to emerge behind the enemy for a finishing blow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Recovery">
    Finish the enemy with the whip to maximize the chance of dropping Joule Jars, ensuring your sidearm is ready for the next threat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The interaction isn't limited to just hitting the weapons. Some sidearms, like poison bombs, can be hit with the whip to launch them further than a standard throw would allow. This "whip-launching" technique is vital for clearing distant hazards or hitting bosses that stay out of normal reach.

| Synergy Type | Sidearm Involved | Interaction Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Redirection** | Gyro Dagger | Resets flight path and increases damage |
| **Detonation** | Explosive/Poison Bombs | Instantly triggers the effect upon whip contact |
| **Extension** | Spear/Dagger | Increases the velocity and range of the projectile |
| **AoE Trigger** | Volt Hatchet | Can spread the shockwave further across conductive surfaces |

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Tactic">
Try throwing the Volt Hatchet over a wall and then burrowing immediately. The shockwave will soften up the enemies on the other side while you remain completely safe underground, allowing for a clean entry into the next room.
</Callout>

Choosing to invest in "Joule Efficiency" early on can allow you to use your sidearms much more frequently, which is often a safer bet for beginners than raw damage.
