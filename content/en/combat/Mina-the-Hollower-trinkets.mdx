<Section icon={Shield} title="Pure Passive Trinkets: Essential Stat Boosters">

For players who prefer a straightforward approach, pure passive trinkets provide flat bonuses to Mina’s core attributes. These are often the most reliable items during the early hours of the game when you are still mastering the timing of the burrow dodge. These trinkets focus on attack power, defense, and sidearm effectiveness.

| Trinket Name | Primary Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lace Glove** | Increases attack power by one level | Whip-focused builds |
| **Twillweed** | Raises defense by one level | New players/Boss fights |
| **Smelling Salts** | Increases sidearm attack power | Ranged/Tactical play |
| **Vial Pouch** | Carry two extra plasma vials | High-difficulty areas |
| **Spark Orb** | Grants one extra spark slot | Resource protection |

While stat boosters may seem "boring" compared to items that cause explosions, they are vital for overcoming the math-heavy challenges of later dungeons. The **Vial Pouch**, in particular, is a top-tier pick because the healing system in Mina the Hollower is inspired by *Bloodborne*, requiring aggressive play to maintain resources.

<Callout type="tip" title="Stacking Benefits">
Don't overlook the Spark Orb. In a game where dying twice without retrieving your spark means losing all your bones, having an extra safety net is often more valuable than a minor damage increase.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Modified Passives: Changing the Game Flow">

Modified passives are where the **Mina the Hollower trinkets** system truly begins to shine. These items don't just buff Mina; they alter her movement, invulnerability frames, and reactions to taking damage. These are essential for players looking to refine their "Hollowing" (burrowing) skills.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Evasion Powder">
    Provides longer **invulnerability time** after taking damage, allowing for safer repositioning during chaotic mob encounters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Iron Lung">
    Increases the **maximum burrow time**, which is crucial for traversing long gaps and staying safe from ground-based projectiles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Steady Souls">
    Reduces **knockback distance** when hit, preventing the frustrating experience of being bumped into pits or spikes.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

These trinkets are particularly effective when combined. For instance, **Iron Lung** paired with **Oozing Organ** (which leaves a damaging trail while burrowed) turns a defensive maneuver into a potent offensive tool.

| Utility Trinket | Mechanical Change | Strategy Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Brisk Brew** | Faster movement after attacking | Hit-and-run tactics |
| **Mandre Root** | Radial scream pushes enemies away | Crowd control |
| **Blood Pressure Bonnet** | More damage at low health | High-risk playstyle |
| **Oozing Organ** | Leaves a damaging ooze trail | Passive damage |
| **Nder Belt** | Radial explosion on surfacing | Offensive burrowing |

<Callout type="warning" title="Burrow Timing">
While Iron Lung allows you to stay underground longer, remember that surfacing into an enemy's hitbox still deals damage unless you have specific offensive surfacing trinkets equipped.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Active Trinkets and Combat Abilities">

Active trinkets require manual input or specific conditions to trigger powerful effects. These items often grant Mina abilities that feel like they were ripped directly from classic handheld *Zelda* or *Castlevania* titles. Mastery of these items is what separates a novice Hollower from a master.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Your Combo">
    Equip the **Combo Capacitor**. This trinket increases your damage output as you land consecutive hits. However, be careful—whiffing an attack resets the bonus to zero.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Air Dash">
    Use the **Bellow's Bustle** to perform an air dash. This is not only a combat tool for closing distances but also essential for reaching high-tier chests tucked away in the rafters of Tenebrous Isle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Seismic Pulses">
    While burrowed, the **Seismic Belt** allows you to press the attack button to emit a damaging pulse. This is perfect for clearing out small enemies like spiders or bats before you surface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Aerial Attacks">
    The **Tumbling Tutu** enables a secondary attack while airborne. This pairs perfectly with the whip's natural range to keep Mina safe while dealing consistent damage from above.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Exploration Synergy">
Combine the **Fairing Wisp** (slow fall) with the **Bellow's Bustle** (air dash) to bypass entire platforming sections. This combo is a favorite for speedrunners and those hunting for every secret.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Bone Management and Currency Conversion">

Acquiring the best **Mina the Hollower trinkets** requires a steady supply of bones. The game utilizes a leveling system reminiscent of *Zelda II*, where you choose to upgrade Health, Attack, or Magic (Sidearms) upon reaching an XP threshold. However, Yacht Club Games has introduced a brilliant twist: **Bonestone Shards**.

| Currency Type | Persistence | Primary Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bones** | Lost on death (if spark fails) | Leveling up stats / Buying trinkets |
| **Bonestone Shards** | **Persistent** after death | Safe long-term saving for expensive items |
| **Sparks** | Recoverable at death site | Protecting current bone held |

When you level up, you have the option to convert your current bone progress into Bonestone Shards. This is a vital strategy if you are about to enter a boss room or a high-risk area. It ensures that your hard-earned progress isn't wiped out by a single mistake.
