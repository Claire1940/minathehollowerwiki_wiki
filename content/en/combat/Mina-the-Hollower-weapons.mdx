### Weapon Upgrade Mechanics

Weapons in Mina the Hollower are upgradeable, with each typically having two possible upgrades. These upgrades are acquired by picking up another instance of the same weapon type after you've already obtained it.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Base Weapon">
    Obtain the initial version of a sidearm (e.g., Dagger, Axe) through drops or shop purchases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Duplicate Weapon">
    Discover another instance of the same weapon type in the world or from defeated enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Upgrade Automatically">
    Upon picking up the duplicate, the weapon will automatically upgrade, granting new abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Trinkets and Bone Up System: Augmenting Your Arsenal">
Beyond the core **Mina the Hollower weapons**, the game features a "Bone Up" leveling system and collectible trinkets that allow for deep customization of Mina's combat style. These elements can significantly boost weapon power and create unique builds tailored to your preferred playstyle.

The Bone Up system uses "Bones" collected from digging or defeating enemies as both currency and experience. You can spend these bones to upgrade core stats like defense and attack, or to augment trinkets and weapons.

### Trinket Types and Effects

Trinkets are small creatures or items that provide unique buffs. They can interact with your weapons and abilities in surprising ways, offering both offensive and defensive advantages.

| Trinket Example | Description | Potential Weapon Synergy | Playstyle Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Spiderling Charm** | Increases poison duration and damage | Extends Poison Bomb effectiveness, more persistent AoE | DoT (Damage over Time) builds, zone control |
| **Bat Wing Pendant** | Grants a chance for projectiles to pierce | Improves Dagger and Spear utility, hitting multiple foes | Multi-target damage, breaking shields |
| **Gator Scale** | Reduces incoming damage while burrowing | Allows aggressive burrowing into enemy groups | Tanky builds, close-quarters combat |
| **Spark Core** | Enhances Battery Buster's charge rate | Faster access to powerful charged shots | High burst damage, quick enemy elimination |
