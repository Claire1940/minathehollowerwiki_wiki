<Section icon={Trophy} title="Understanding Bonestone in Mina the Hollower">
In Mina the Hollower, **Bonestone** serves as a critical resource for your progression, acting as a condensed and safeguarded form of the primary currency, Bones. While Bones are acquired from defeating enemies and breaking objects, they are vulnerable to loss upon defeat. **Bonestone** provides a strategic layer, allowing players to secure their hard-earned currency.

When you collect enough Bones, Mina can use her journal to "bone up" on various categories like attack or defense, essentially leveling up her stats. The game's healing system also ties into combat aggression, similar to a "Bloodborne-like rally mechanic," where attacking enemies can build a yellow meter that can be converted into health. This aggressive playstyle often means facing risks, making **Bonestone** management even more vital.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Acquiring and Utilizing Bonestone">
**Bonestone** isn't just a safety net; it's an integral part of Mina's economic system. You'll encounter Bonestone as special drops, often appearing as yellow diamonds, which are essentially compressed Bones. The primary hub for managing this resource is Mina's Underlab, where the Bone Stone Machine resides. This machine is your key to converting Bonestone into usable Bones and vice-versa, allowing for strategic financial planning.

The game encourages aggressive play, where building a "rally" meter by attacking enemies can restore health. This aggressive approach can lead to more frequent encounters and potential defeats. Having a robust **Bonestone** reserve ensures that even if you face setbacks, your overall progress in terms of upgrades isn't severely hindered.

### Bonestone Acquisition Methods

| **Enemy Drops**  | Rare chance to drop from defeated enemies.                                  | Low       | Variable  |

<Callout type="warning" title="Risk Management">
While Bonestone is safe from loss on death, the Bones you currently carry are not. Always consider returning to the Underlab or a checkpoint to convert your Bones into Bonestone, especially before challenging areas or bosses.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Bone Stone Machine: Your Financial Hub">
The Bone Stone Machine, located in Mina's Underlab, is the central point for interacting with your **Bonestone**. This machine allows you to convert your collected Bonestone back into Bones, which can then be used for upgrades, purchasing items like small keys, or increasing Mina's core stats. It's designed to give players control over their economy, enabling them to make informed decisions about when to save and when to spend.

The developers have designed the death system to have stakes without being overly punishing. While you lose Bones if you die without a Spark of Life, having **Bonestone** banked ensures that a portion of your wealth is always secure. This system balances risk and reward, allowing for progression even through challenging sections.

### Using the Bone Stone Machine

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Underlab">
    Travel to Mina's Underlab, which serves as your main hub and checkpoint. This is where you'll find the Bone Stone Machine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Machine">
    Interact with the Bone Stone Machine. You'll see your current Bones and any banked Bonestone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Convert Bonestone">
    Select the option to convert Bonestone into Bones. You can choose to convert all or a specific amount, depending on your immediate needs. This will transfer the value of Bonestone into your active Bone currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Spend Your Bones">
    Once converted, use your newly acquired Bones to purchase upgrades for Mina's stats (attack, defense), buy sidearms, trinkets, or essential items like small keys from vendors.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Strategic Spending">
Upgrading your defense or attack with Bones from Bonestone conversion also fully restores Mina's health. This can be a crucial tactical move in dire situations, even if you weren't planning an immediate upgrade.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Upgrades and Progression with Bones">
Once you have converted your **Bonestone** into Bones, the true power of this currency comes into play. Bones are used to enhance Mina's capabilities, making her stronger, more resilient, and better equipped to handle the challenges of Tenebrous Isle. From increasing her attack power to bolstering her defenses, every Bone you spend contributes to Mina's growth.

The system is designed to be flexible, allowing you to prioritize upgrades based on your playstyle and the challenges you face. Whether you prefer a more aggressive approach with higher attack or a cautious one with increased defense, Bones provide the means to tailor Mina to your preferences.
