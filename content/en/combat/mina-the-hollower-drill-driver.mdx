<Section icon={Swords} title="Drill Driver in Combat: Facing Secret Bosses">
While primarily a traversal tool, the **Mina the Hollower Drill Driver** also plays a significant role in combat, particularly against certain secret bosses. Its unique properties can be leveraged to gain an advantage in otherwise tough fights.

Two notable secret bosses where the Drill Driver proves invaluable are Evra and Thalassion.

### Evra Encounter Strategy

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup">
    Equip the **Drill Driver** and Keri the Wisp. Ensure you have the Brisk Brew. Traverse the Courtyard gap using the Drill Driver.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Entering the Dungeon">
    Use three Plasma Vials to open the gate to the right in the mini-dungeon. Explore until you find Evra in a grave.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Combat Tactics">
    Evra charges and fires skeletons. **Burrow often** to avoid her massive undead steed and skeletal projectiles. Focus on whittling down the skeleton numbers to create safe dodging spaces.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Victory Reward">
    Defeat Evra to receive a valuable **Health Rose**, significantly boosting your survivability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Thalassion Encounter Strategy

Thalassion, the giant fish boss, is another adversary where the **Drill Driver** can make a difference, especially in the approach.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Fishing Quest Completion">
    Obtain the Fishing Rod from Irwin and Durwin. Catch 15 fish from each area, leaving one in Backwater.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Approaching Thalassion">
    Return to Irwin and Durwin's hangout. Equip both the Fishing Rod and the **Drill Driver** (or use the raft). Head right from Durwin to the southeast corner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Climbing the Gap">
    Use your Fishing Rod on the slime above to climb the gap. Continue to the next area to find Thalassion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Boss Battle">
    The fighting arena is small. Choose your attack windows carefully. Be conservative with attacks, as Thalassion's waves and charging bites deal heavy damage. Frequent dodging is key.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reward">
    Upon defeat, talk to Irwin, then Durwin, to complete the fish collection and earn the **Gilded Rod upgrade**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
