<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Hollower's Shield Combat Mechanics">

The Hollower's Shield is defined by its versatility. It is a sturdy tool that doubles as a melee weapon, allowing Mina to maintain a "bulwark" playstyle. Mastering this weapon requires understanding the frame data of the bash and the timing of the parry window.

### Core Move Set

The shield's effectiveness is tied to how long you interact with the attack button. Unlike the Whip, which locks you in place, the shield allows for minor positional adjustments during its active frames.

| Command | Action | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tap [Attack]** | Shield Bash | A quick forward strike that deals moderate knockback. |
| **Hold [Attack]** | Block | Mina raises the shield, reducing incoming damage from the front. |
| **Timed Bash** | Parry | Bashing exactly as an enemy strike connects stuns the foe. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Blocking is not infinite. While the game doesn't feature a traditional stamina bar, holding a block for too long against heavy enemies can result in a guard break, leaving Mina vulnerable to follow-up hits.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock the Hollower's Shield">

You will not find the shield in the opening hold of the ship. During the tutorial, you are limited to the Whisper and Vesper daggers, the Nightstar flail, or the Blaststrike Maul. To obtain the **Mina the Hollower shield**, you must progress through the initial Ossex chapters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Prologue">
    Navigate the tutorial ship and defeat the initial scripted encounters to reach the town of Ossex.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit Legovich the Blacksmith">
    Locate the orange-red building west of the central fountain in Ossex. Legovich sells additional weapons once you have cleared the first major dungeon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat Armand">
    To unlock the full inventory, you must defeat Legovich's brother, Armand, in the basement. Use the burrowing mechanic to lead him into lava tiles to lower his defense.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase with Bones">
    The first new weapon costs 2,000 bones. Subsequent weapons, including the shields, increase to 2,500 bones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Alternative Path">
If you prefer to skip the Armand boss fight, head to the back-alley shop west of the main weapon store. You will need the **Umbrella** or **Iron Steed** to cross the gap. Prices here start at 5,000 bones, but you can acquire the Guardian Casket early.
</Callout>

</Section>
