| Mechanic       | Description                                                                                             | Strategic Use                                                                                             |
| :------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Burrowing**  | Dive underground, becoming invulnerable and gaining a burst of speed.                                   | Evade attacks, traverse gaps, surprise enemies, extend jumps.                                             |
| **Whip (Nightstar)** | Mina's primary weapon, offering decent range and quickness.                                             | Standard attack, ideal for crowd control and hitting enemies from a safe distance.                        |
| **Sidearms**   | Secondary weapons (like daggers or hammers) that use jewels.                                            | Provides varied attack patterns, elemental damage, or utility; crucial for specific enemy types or bosses. |
| **Trinkets**   | Equipable items that grant passive effects or special abilities.                                         | Customize Mina's abilities, such as reviving after death or creating tiles on ledges.                     |
| **Health Recovery** | Health vials refill based on damage dealt, encouraging aggressive play.                                  | Manage risk vs. reward; fight strategically to earn back health.                                          |
