<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering the Hollower Mechanics">

The defining feature of the game is Mina's ability to "hollow" or burrow into the ground. This isn't just a traversal tool; it is a core survival mechanic that differentiates this title from other top-down adventures. Mastering the timing of your burrow is the difference between life and death in the demo's more challenging sections.

**Key Mechanics of Hollowing:**
- **Invulnerability**: While underground, Mina is immune to most surface-level attacks and projectiles.
- **Traversal**: Burrowing allows you to pass under low walls, through small gaps, and over certain hazards like spiked floors.
- **Momentum**: Jumping and then holding the burrow button allows Mina to dive forward, maintaining her speed and extending her reach.
- **Resource Recovery**: Breaking objects in the environment often yields "Bones," the game's primary currency used for purchasing upgrades and items in the Power's Lab.

| Action | Control (Switch) | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attack** | Y Button | Uses equipped primary weapon |
| **Jump** | B Button | Standard leap to avoid ground hazards |
| **Hollow** | Hold B (In Air) | Burrows into the ground |
| **Use Sidearm** | X Button | Consumes Spark to use secondary item |
| **Heal** | R Button | Consumes Plasma Vial to restore HP |
