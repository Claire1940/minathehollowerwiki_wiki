<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Mina's Evasion Mechanics">
In Mina the Hollower, dodging isn't about a dedicated dodge roll button but rather a clever use of Mina's core abilities. Her primary evasion tool is the burrow mechanic, which allows her to dive underground, becoming invulnerable to most attacks and moving quickly across the terrain. While burrowing offers excellent protection, its animation time means it needs to be used proactively. Reacting too late to an incoming attack by attempting to burrow can still result in taking damage. Therefore, understanding enemy behaviors and anticipating their moves is paramount.

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Enemy Attack Patterns">
Many players find certain enemies, especially charging ones, difficult to avoid. The key to effective dodging in Mina the Hollower lies in observing and learning these attack patterns. Enemies often telegraph their moves with distinct animations or wind-up phases.

For charging enemies, instead of trying to burrow *after* they've started their charge, try to anticipate it. Keep a safe distance, bait their attack, and then burrow or move away during their wind-up. This allows you to avoid the hit and potentially counter-attack when they are vulnerable.

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware of Animation Lock">
Attempting to burrow immediately after attacking can leave Mina vulnerable during the burrow animation. Prioritize evasion over immediate follow-up attacks if an enemy is about to strike.
</Callout>
