<Section icon={Trophy} title="Managing Bones and Progression">
**Bones** are a key resource in Mina the Hollower, acting as both currency and experience points. When Mina is defeated, she loses her collected bones, which are left at the spot of her demise. Players can retrieve these lost bones by returning to the location, similar to mechanics found in Souls-like games. However, repeated deaths before retrieval will result in permanent loss. This system adds another layer to understanding **Mina the Hollower how to save** and manage risks.

<Callout type="warning" title="Bone Loss Risk">
Be mindful of your bone count, especially after clearing a difficult section. If you die again before retrieving your lost bones, they will be gone forever. Prioritize reaching an Underlab or Spark Restorator to crystallize them.
</Callout>
