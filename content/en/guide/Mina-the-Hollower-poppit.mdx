<Section icon={Map} title="Eastern Heath Poppit Location Guide">
Your first encounter with Poppit is likely to be in the Eastern Heath, an area that demands careful navigation through environmental hazards. This location is relatively straightforward to reach from Ossex, making it an excellent starting point for your search.

<Callout type="warning" title="Environmental Hazards">
The Eastern Heath features moving spikes and laser turrets. Approach with caution and time your movements precisely to avoid unnecessary damage.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Exit Ossex East">
    Leave Ossex via the eastern exit and proceed into the Eastern Heath.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate Spikes and Pits">
    Immediately head straight down after passing through the walkway. You'll enter an area filled with moving spikes and pits requiring precise jumps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cross Floating Platforms">
    At the bottom of this screen, head right. Jump across the floating platform, dodging laser turrets as you move further right.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate Hidden Entrance">
    In the next screen, look for a stone fence in the bottom-right corner. It's marked by two bluish torches. Burrow inside to find Poppit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon reaching this first shop, Poppit will offer the following wares:

| Item           | Price (Bones) | Description                                                |
| :------------- | :------------ | :--------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Willow the Wisp** | 400           | After taking damage, summon a friendly wisp that boosts your attack damage and speed. |
| **Kear (x1)**       | 200           | Opens locked doors, blocks, or chests.                     |

</Section>

<Section icon={Compass} title="Southern Outskirts Poppit Location Guide">
The Southern Outskirts present a more perilous journey than the Eastern Heath, with tougher enemies and intricate environmental puzzles. However, with quick reflexes, many of the threats near Poppit's shop entrance can be bypassed.

<Callout type="success" title="Strategic Movement">
While the Southern Outskirts are dangerous, many tough enemies can be dodged or bypassed if you maintain momentum and focus on reaching the objective.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Southern Outskirts">
    From Ossex, head into the Southern Outskirts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate Cave Network">
    Go down the first stairway, then immediately east. You'll find a large pit with rebels and a floating purple puff bush. Leap onto the bush to reach a high ledge and enter a Cave Network.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Traverse Puff Bushes">
    Inside the cave, navigate a series of floating purple puff bushes while fending off bats. Go left, then down, and back to the right to find an exit near your entry point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manipulate Puff Bush">
    Back outside, head down and left to find a rebel guarding another purple puff bush. Burrow underneath it and push it left to the far side of the area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reach High Ledge">
    Position the puff bush in front of a ledge with hedges to the south. Jump off the bush to reach this high ledge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Find Hidden Shop">
    On the high ledge, past strong birds and a large Rebel, look for an opening between two torches guarded by red birds. Burrow inside to find Poppit's second shop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

At this location, Poppit offers a crucial item for your progression:

| Item        | Price (Bones) | Description                               |
| :---------- | :------------ | :---------------------------------------- |
| **Kear (x1)** | 300           | Opens locked doors, blocks, or chests.    |

<Section icon={Target} title="Western Wilds Poppit Location Guide">
The final Poppit location is in the Western Wilds, an area characterized by tough enemies and lava puzzles. It's advisable to complete the first dungeon before venturing here due to the increased difficulty. This area requires creative use of Mina's burrowing abilities to manipulate the environment.

<Callout type="info" title="Lava Mechanics">
In the Western Wilds, lava pools expand when you burrow in adjacent tiles. This mechanic is essential for breaking red ore and revealing hidden paths.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Western Wilds">
    If the western Ossex entrance is locked, travel down from the Southern city entrance, head left after the stairs, and then up toward the Occupied Bridge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Create Lava Path (Under Bridge)">
    The area around the Occupied Bridge has lava pools. Burrow next to them to expand the lava and break red ore. Create a path left below the bridge, leading to a trail of lava that goes up to a path under the bridge's northern side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter Molten Foundry">
    Follow this path up and to the right to enter the Molten Foundry.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Burrow Under Foundry Floor">
    In the Foundry, ignore the right doorway (which leads back to Ossex). Instead, check the top-right of the room for an open grate. Burrow into it and under the floor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Illuminate Dark Area">
    Travel under the locked door on the Foundry's left side, then upward into a darkened area with a small trail of lava at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Reveal Poppit's Shop">
    Use the lava to create a trail of light leading left, through a tunnel with pits and red ore. Continue digging a path for the lava to follow to the top of this dark room. This will illuminate a cracked wall. Attack the wall to reveal Poppit's final location.
  </Step>
</Steps>

At Poppit's third and final shop, you can acquire another invaluable wisp:

Finding all three Poppit locations and acquiring his wares is a significant achievement in Mina the Hollower, bringing you closer to understanding the game's deepest secrets.
