<Section icon={Target} title="Mina the Hollower sandfalls: Step-by-Step Crossing">

Mastering the wide pit jump involves a specific sequence of inputs. This technique leverages Mina's unique "Hollower" ability to burrow into the ground and launch herself with momentum. Follow these steps to clear the gap in the Sifted Sands.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Launch Point">
    Approach the edge of the Wide Pit in the Sifted Sands. Look for the soft sand patches that allow Mina to burrow. Positioning is key; you want to be as close to the edge as possible before starting your burrow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Burrow and Charge">
    Press the burrow button to submerge Mina. Move toward the edge while underground. This builds the necessary momentum for the launch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Release and Launch">
    Release the burrow button exactly as Mina reaches the ledge. This will propel her into the air. The height of this launch is determined by how long you were submerged and your movement speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deploy the Umbrella">
    While in mid-air, at the apex of your jump, press the action button to open the Yellow Umbrella. This will slow your descent and initiate a glide.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Trigger Auto-Jump">
    As you approach the far side of the sandfalls, the umbrella will automatically trigger a secondary jump upon landing. This final boost provides the distance needed to reach solid ground.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Input Phase | Action | Timing Window | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | Burrow | Pre-launch | Momentum Build |
| Phase 2 | Release | Edge of Pit | Vertical Launch |
| Phase 3 | Action | Apex of Jump | Glide/Umbrella |
| Phase 4 | Contact | Landing | Auto-Jump |

<Callout type="warning" title="Timing Warning">
Releasing the burrow button too early will result in a standard jump that lacks the distance to clear the pit. Ensure you wait until the very edge of the platform to release for maximum trajectory.
