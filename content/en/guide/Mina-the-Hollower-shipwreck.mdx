<Section icon={Target} title="Accessing the Shipwreck in Loner's Landing">
Reaching the **Mina the Hollower shipwreck** requires a specific sequence of actions and items. Players must gather three kears (keys) and navigate through several areas to uncover this hidden weapon cache.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Three Kears">
    Before embarking on your journey to the shipwreck, ensure you have collected **three kears**. These keys are essential for unlocking the path to the ship. Kears can be found throughout Tenebrous Isle by defeating enemies, opening chests, or purchasing them from merchants.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter Shanty Swamp: Backwaters">
    From Ossex, head west to enter the **Shanty Swamp: Backwaters** area. This region acts as a gateway to the Loner's Landing beach where the shipwreck resides.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire Pinky's Ladder">
    Within the Shanty Swamp: Backwaters, you must locate and **steal Pinky's ladder**. This ladder is crucial for climbing a specific ledge on your route.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate to Loner's Landing Beach">
    Leave the large lake area with circling platforms to the southeast, passing the train station. Continue walking down to the **Loner's Landing beach**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Climb and Burrow to Enter the Ship">
    Use Pinky's ladder to climb the designated ledge. From there, jump across a series of barrels and then **burrow through the damaged hull** of the ship to gain entry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Pre-Requisite for Access">
The shipwreck area in Loner's Landing is only accessible after you have made your first visit to Ossex. Attempting to reach it earlier will be fruitless as the necessary paths will not be open.
</Callout>

</Section>
