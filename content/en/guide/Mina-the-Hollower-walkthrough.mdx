Combat in Mina the Hollower is a dance of positioning and timing. Unlike traditional action-adventurers, Mina can burrow beneath the earth. **Burrowing is defense, not just exploration.** It allows you to bypass environmental hazards like flames or spikes and move safely beneath large monsters when a corridor becomes too tight.

Choosing the right weapon early in your **Mina the Hollower walkthrough** will define your survival rate. For beginners, the **Nightstar** is the superior choice. Its range allows you to observe enemy telegraphs without standing in the immediate danger zone.

| Combat Action | Primary Use | Expert Advice |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Progression: Bones, Joules, and Trinkets">

Progression in Mina the Hollower follows a "risk vs. reward" structure similar to souls-like games. The primary currency is **Bones**, which are used to "Bone Up" (level up) your stats. If you carry too many Bones into an unknown area and die, you risk losing your accumulated wealth.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Bones">
    Defeat enemies and break objects to gather Bones. Avoid hoarding them for too long.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find a Bone Up Station">
    Locate a checkpoint to invest your Bones into Attack, Defense, or Sidearm stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Trinkets">
    Find or buy Trinkets to add passive buffs. You have 60 to discover in total.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Joules">
    Collect Joules to power your Sidearms. Remember: Sidearms are lost upon death.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When deciding which stats to prioritize, evaluate your recent deaths. If you find that fights are dragging on and you are making mistakes due to fatigue, raise your **Attack**. If you are being two-shot by basic mobs, invest heavily in **Defense**.

| Issue | Recommended Stat | Expected Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Frequent Death** | Defense | Increased survivability and learning time |
| **Slow Clearing** | Attack | Fewer enemy cycles and faster boss kills |
| **Resource Starved** | Sidearm | Better utility and ranged options |
| **Positioning Errors** | Trinkets | Custom buffs to dodge or burrow speed |

<Callout type="success" title="Build Strategy">
Trinkets make your build flexible. If you are struggling with a specific boss, swap out your Trinkets to counter their specific damage type or movement pattern. Never change your entire build after one death; change one Trinket at a time to test its effectiveness.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="The 6 Spark Generators Walkthrough">

The core of any **Mina the Hollower walkthrough** is the journey to the 6 Spark Generators. These act as the primary milestones of the game. Tenebrous Isle is non-linear, meaning you can often choose which branch to tackle first. However, if a route feels too draining, it is better to leave and explore a different path than to force a "bad wall."

1.  **Enter a new room at mid-range**: Don't rush into the center.
2.  **Observe first**: Watch how the enemies move and what environmental hazards are present.
3.  **Clear the threats**: Focus on ranged enemies first, then melee.
4.  **Resource Check**: Only use Sidearms like the **Recall Disc** or **Gyro Dagger** if the room presents a clear threat you cannot handle with your main weapon.
5.  **Scavenge**: Break every object *after* the fight is over to look for Joule Jars and extra Bones.

| Area Type | Difficulty | Main Hazard | Recommended Tool |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Overworld** | Low | Patrols | Nightstar |
| **Crypts** | Medium | Tight Corridors | Burrowing |
| **Industrial** | High | Spikes/Flames | Sidearms |
| **Boss Arenas** | Extreme | Pattern Overlap | Trinket Synergy |

<Callout type="info" title="Exploration Tip">
A "fair wall" is a boss you are still learning. A "bad wall" is a route that empties your health and resources before you even reach the boss. If you arrive at a Spark Generator with no health, go back and find a more efficient route.
</Callout>
