<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Mina's Abilities in the Docks">

Mina's unique abilities are vital for navigating the Blighted Docks. Her "Hollowing" technique allows her to burrow underground, granting temporary invincibility and quick traversal. This is particularly useful for crossing gaps, dodging attacks, and bypassing groups of enemies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Utilizing Hollowing for Traversal">
    When faced with wide gaps or hazardous ground, activate **Hollowing** to burrow underneath. This makes Mina invincible and allows her to resurface on safe ground. Practice timing this for optimal efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hollowing for Evasion">
    During boss fights or against large groups of enemies, **Hollowing** can be used to quickly dodge incoming attacks. Burrow just before an attack lands and resurface behind the enemy for a counter-attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimizing Nightstar Whip Attacks">
    The **Nightstar whip** can attack in four directions. Experiment with different attack angles to hit enemies effectively, especially those with specific weak points or flying patterns. Combine with movement for hit-and-run tactics.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Sidearm Usage">
    Mina can carry one sidearm at a time, such as hatchets or daggers. For the Blighted Docks, consider sidearms that offer ranged attacks for the Swamp Lurkers or area-of-effect for the Toxic Slimes. Switch sidearms based on the immediate threat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Hollowing for Hidden Paths">
Sometimes, Hollowing can reveal hidden passages or shortcuts that are otherwise inaccessible. Always try burrowing in suspicious areas or near seemingly impassable obstacles.
</Callout>
