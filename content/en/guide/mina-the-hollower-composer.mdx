<Section icon={Target} title="Inspiration and Gothic Horror Themes">

The musical direction was heavily influenced by the developers' desire to create a darker experience than their previous titles. The **mina the hollower composer** staff looked toward classic horror titles and Disneyland attractions to find the right balance of "scary but fun." This is evident in the transition from the upbeat exploration themes of Tenebrous Isle to the dread-inducing tracks found in the deeper caverns.

**Key Influences on the Soundtrack:**
- **Castlevania**: The driving, rhythmic percussion and minor-key melodies.
- **The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening**: The whimsical yet occasionally melancholic overworld themes.
- **Bloodborne**: The sense of cosmic dread and Victorian/Gothic atmosphere translated into 8-bit.
- **The Haunted Mansion**: Playful, spooky motifs that prevent the game from feeling overly oppressive.
