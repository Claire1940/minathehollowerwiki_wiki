<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies and Tips">
The Deprived Path is designed to be challenging. Success hinges on a combination of combat prowess, environmental awareness, and strategic planning.

### Combat and Movement
- **Master Burrowing:** Mina's burrow ability is not just for movement; it's a crucial defensive tool. Use it to dodge attacks, pass through enemies, and avoid environmental hazards like spikes.
- **Weapon Choice:** While the axe is a solid all-rounder, consider experimenting with other weapons or sidearms to find what best suits your playstyle against the unique enemies of the Deprived Path.
- **Ricochet Attacks:** Some enemies or environmental elements can be hit indirectly. Practice ricocheting your attacks to hit targets from a safe distance or around corners.

### Resource Management
- **Health Vials:** These are your lifeline. Use them when critically low on health, or before a known difficult section. The health vial relic that restores both health and plasma is particularly powerful.
- **Plasma:** Manage your plasma for sidearm usage and special abilities. Some relics, like the Desperation Bonnet, can make sidearms more cost-effective.
- **Money (Bonestone Gems):** Collect as much as possible. Money can be used to purchase upgrades, new relics, and consumables from merchants found throughout Tenebrous Isle.
