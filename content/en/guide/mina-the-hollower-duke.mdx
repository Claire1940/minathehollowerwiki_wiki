<Section icon={Map} title="Locating the Duke in Mina the Hollower">
Finding the Duke is a key step in this particular side quest that offers both rewards and narrative implications. As you progress through _Mina the Hollower_, you'll encounter various characters and choices that shape your journey. The Duke's location is tied to a specific area where you'll need to overcome environmental obstacles.

You'll find yourself in a chamber with an out-of-reach Underlab entrance and a barrier requiring two plasma vials to remove. The Duke awaits on the other side of this barrier. Once the barrier is clear, make your way up the rope and across to reach him. He will then ask to accompany you. While he won't directly assist in combat, he will follow you. Be mindful that if he takes damage from enemies or environmental hazards like spike rollers, he'll panic and stop following until you walk into him again. This area also offers an excellent respawn point for an upcoming boss fight once you unlock the Underlab at the end of the path.

<Callout type="tip">
**Always clear the path ahead** when escorting the Duke. His fragile nature means he'll flee if damaged, making progression more cumbersome. Prioritize removing threats before he enters a new area.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="The Choice: To Seal or Not to Seal the Duke">
The central dilemma concerning the **Mina the Hollower Duke** revolves around whether to fulfill his wish and seal him in the family crypt. This choice presents a clear trade-off between an immediate, powerful reward and a minor narrative consequence later in the game.

The Duke explicitly asks you to place him within his family's crypt, desiring to be with his beloved forever. If you choose to honor his request, you will immediately receive the **Proto Spark trinket**. This highly useful item grants you a free revive, bringing you back from death once your health reaches zero without consuming one of your precious sparks. This can be a game-changer in challenging encounters.

Conversely, if you decide against sealing him and simply walk away, you will not receive the Proto Spark trinket. While the Duke does eventually pass away regardless of your choice, the trinket is only acquired by actively sealing him.

<Callout type="warning" title="Spoiler Alert">
The decision to seal the Duke does have a narrative repercussion later in the game. After restoring all six spark generators and returning to the Radiant Manor, Lionel and his followers will list your perceived misdeeds, including "burying the Duke alive." This is purely a storytelling element and does not impact gameplay or mechanics.
</Callout>
