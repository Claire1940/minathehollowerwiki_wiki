Mina's combat is highly dynamic, blending traditional action-adventure elements with unique mechanics like the plasma bar and burrowing.

Beyond direct combat, Mina the Hollower incorporates environmental elements and platforming challenges that often dictate how you approach enemy encounters. Learning to use these to your advantage is crucial.

### Environmental Hazards & Enemy Synergy

Each region's enemies are designed to interact with its unique environment. For instance, pigmen in industrial areas might slam the ground to activate lava floors, while giant plants in swamps can fill areas with hazardous spikes.

<Callout type="success" title="Leverage the Environment">
Always look for ways to use the environment against enemies. Pushing enemies into pits, luring them into hazardous zones, or using platforming to create openings can turn tough encounters into manageable ones.
</Callout>

### Mastering Movement and Attack Combinations

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Observe and Learn">
    Spend a moment to watch enemy patterns and environmental traps. Identify safe zones and attack windows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Combine Burrowing with Attacks">
    Burrow to evade incoming attacks, then emerge with a strike. For charging enemies, burrow behind them as they pass.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Sidearms and Trinkets">
    Experiment with different sidearms (throwing axes, swords) and trinkets. A trinket allowing Mina to walk on spikes, for example, can dramatically change how you approach spike-filled areas with enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Plasma Vials Strategically">
    Replenish plasma vials at Underlabs, which act as checkpoints. Don't be afraid to use them during boss fights or tough enemy gauntlets, but always try to fill your plasma bar first.
  </Step>
</Steps>
