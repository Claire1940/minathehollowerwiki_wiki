<Section icon={Map} title="The Road to Ossex: Hulk Trooper Mini-Boss">

Your journey to the first major boss takes you through Loner's Landing and the Boardwalk. Before reaching the city of Ossex, you will face a formidable gatekeeper: the **Hulk Trooper**. While not the "main" first boss, he serves as a mandatory skill check that many players find difficult if they haven't prepared.

The Hulk Trooper utilizes a massive club and features two primary phases. He will often jump toward you for a ground slam or perform a charged swing that teleports him forward. The arena is littered with rocks; use these to break his line of sight and absorb the impact of his heavy swings.

| Attack Name | Description | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jumping Slam** | Jumps high and slams club down | Run away or burrow to avoid the AoE |
| **Charged Swing** | Teleports forward with a wide arc | Hide behind rocks or dodge behind him |
| **Standard Strike** | Quick horizontal swipe | Stay at max weapon range to poke |

<Callout type="warning" title="Level Up First">
Do not fight the Hulk Trooper at Level 1. Spend time in the Boardwalk area to farm **Bones** and "Bone Up" at a checkpoint. Increasing your **Attack** to Level 2 makes this fight significantly shorter.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Beat Thorne: The First Boss Guide">

Once you enter Ossex and navigate the Radiant Manor, you will be confronted by **Thorne** in the courtyard. This is officially the **mina the hollower first boss** encounter. Thorne is a rival warrior who is faster and more mobile than any enemy you have faced thus far. He uses a spear-like weapon and commands elemental tornadoes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Watch the Shadow">
    Thorne's most common move is a high jump. While he is off-screen, watch the circular shadow on the ground. This indicates exactly where he will land.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Dodge the Shockwave">
    When Thorne lands, he creates a purple shockwave or summons tornadoes. Do not stand near the impact zone. Wait for the effect to dissipate before moving in.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Two-Hit Rule">
    After Thorne lands and finishes his AoE attack, he is stationary for approximately 2 seconds. Move in, land **two hits**, and immediately retreat. Greed is the primary cause of death in this fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Avoid the Purple Trail">
    Thorne will occasionally dash around, leaving a trail of purple dots. These dots explode after a short delay. Focus entirely on movement during this phase and do not attempt to attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Elemental Phase">
    When Thorne's health drops below 50%, he will summon fire tornadoes that track your movement. Run in a wide circle around the arena to outpace them until the phase ends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Thorne's Move | Danger Level | Best Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High Jump** | High | Track shadow, stay 3 tiles away |
| **Fire Tornado** | Extreme | Continuous movement in a circle |
| **Explosive Trail** | Medium | Stay clear of purple floor markers |
| **Spear Thrust** | Low | Side-step and counter-attack |

<Callout type="success" title="Aggression Rewards">
Remember that hitting the boss refills your Plasma meter. If you are low on health, landing a successful combo is often better than running away, as it allows you to use your Plasma Vial again sooner.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Combat Tactics">

Mastering the **mina the hollower first boss** is just the beginning. To survive the later stages of the game, you must integrate Mina's burrowing ability into your offensive rotations. Burrowing isn't just for movement; it's a defensive tool that grants brief invulnerability frames (i-frames).

When Thorne or the Hulk Trooper performs a wide-reaching swing, you can burrow into the ground to let the attack pass over you. Pop up immediately after the hit-box clears to land a "Burrow Attack," which often deals more damage than a standard swing.

| Mechanic | Benefit | Best Used Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Burrow Dodge** | I-frames / Invulnerability | Wide sweeps and shockwaves |
| **Subweapon Swap** | Versatility | Flying enemies (Daggers) / Bosses (Axe) |
| **Stat Grinding** | Power scaling | Any boss that kills you in 3 hits or less |

<Callout type="tip" title="Stat Priority">
Prioritize **Attack** first to end fights faster, then **Defense** to survive mistakes. Only invest in **Subweapon** stats once your primary weapon is at least Level 4.
</Callout>

</Section>
