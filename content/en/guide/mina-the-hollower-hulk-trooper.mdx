<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding the Hulk Trooper Mini-Boss">
The **Hulk Trooper** is a significant early challenge in Mina the Hollower, appearing as a mini-boss in the Southern Outskirts of the Prologue. This larger, stronger variant of the Shock Trooper boasts impressive size and mobility, allowing it to cover considerable ground and pose a substantial threat. Successfully defeating it is crucial for progressing through the game's initial stages towards Ossex.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Grinding for Success">
Many players find the Hulk Trooper challenging, especially at lower levels. It's highly recommended to grind for Bones and use the **Bone Up** system to increase your Attack and Defense stats before engaging this mini-boss. Even a single level in Attack and Defense can make a significant difference in the fight's difficulty.
</Callout>

The Hulk Trooper has a distinct health bar at the bottom of the screen, indicating a more complex encounter than standard enemies. Familiarizing yourself with its attack patterns and weaknesses will be key to overcoming this early hurdle.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Strategies for Defeating the Hulk Trooper">
Defeating the **Hulk Trooper** requires a combination of careful positioning, timely attacks, and smart use of your abilities and upgrades.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Stat Upgrades">
    Before the fight, locate a checkpoint and grind nearby enemies for Bones. Use the **Bone Up** system to increase your Attack and Defense. Focus on Attack first for faster fights, then Defense for survivability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Environmental Cover">
    The arena often contains rocks. Use these to your advantage by hiding behind them to block the Hulk Trooper's charged swing attacks. This creates openings for you to safely reposition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Exploit Recovery Windows">
    After the Hulk Trooper performs a charged attack, it experiences a brief recovery period. This is your primary window to strike with your primary weapon. Get in one or two hits, then retreat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deploy Secondary Weapons">
    Don't forget your secondary weapons, like the **Gyro Daggers**. These can deal damage from a distance while you are evading or waiting for an opening, adding consistent chip damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Master the Burrow Ability">
    Mina's burrow ability is crucial for evasion. Use it to quickly move under the Hulk Trooper's attacks or to reposition yourself behind cover. Burrowing can also extend your jump distance for navigating the environment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Victory Condition">
Once the Hulk Trooper's health bar is depleted, the spikes at the back of the arena will drop, clearing your path forward to Ossex. This mini-boss serves as a significant test of your early-game character progression and combat skills.
</Callout>

</Section>
