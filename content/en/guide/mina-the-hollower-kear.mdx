<Section icon={Search} title="Understanding the Mina the Hollower Kear System">

The **Mina the Hollower Kear** serves as one of the most important collectible currencies in the game. Unlike standard keys that might open a single door in a dungeon, Kears are a universal resource. Finding a Kear allows you to bypass obstacles, reach new sub-areas, and obtain powerful Trinkets that are otherwise inaccessible. Visually, these items resemble a traditional key but feature a distinct flower-shaped handle, fitting the gothic-botanical aesthetic of the world.

<Section icon={Map} title="Regional Kear Locations: Loner's Landing & Ossex">

The early game areas contain several "free" Kears that players should prioritize. These are often hidden behind minor combat encounters or simple platforming challenges.

### Loner's Landing Locations
Loner's Landing serves as the introductory area where you will learn the basics of burrowing and jumping to reach collectibles.

| Location | Specific Spot | How to Obtain |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blighted Docks** | Side cave | Located near the first locked gate |
| **Boardwalk** | South of Outskirts | Use puff shroom to jump across fire |
| **Residence** | Trooper encounter | Defeat enemies and circle clockwise |

### Ossex City & High Street
Ossex is the central hub and contains the highest density of Kears, including several tied to unique NPC interactions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Hollower's Guild">
    Burrow into the entrance in the City Center. Go behind the desk to find a chest on the right.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Telescope Puzzle">
    On Ossex High Street, head north past the gate. Use the telescope to zoom in on the red planet. A comet will drop a Kear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Fireplace Secret">
    In the house west of the Trinket Store, burrow into the fireplace. Follow the long path through the Southern Outskirts to the end.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Hidden Interaction">
In the house on the far western side of Ossex High Street, speak to the woman by the fireplace. If she has seen you donating Bones to the poor on the street, she will give you a Kear for free!
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Areas: Eastern Heath & Astral Orrery">

As you progress into the mid-to-late game, Kear locations become significantly more complex, involving environmental traps and portal-based puzzles.

### Eastern Heath Exploration
The Eastern Heath requires careful timing to navigate the saw traps. This area also houses a secret shop that offers more than just standard gear.

| Area | Feature | Acquisition Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Poppit Shop** | Purple cavern | Buy from Poppit near the saw traps |
| **Saw Traps** | Vertical saws | Navigate south from Ossex bridge |

### Astral Orrery Secrets
The Astral Orrery is a late-game zone that utilizes verticality and portals. You will need to master your burrowing and jumping mechanics to secure these.

| Item | Location | Puzzle Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kear 1** | Astral Orrery | Breakable surface near laser turret |
| **Kear 2** | Astral Orrery | Jump over purple portals to the west |

<Callout type="info" title="Laser Turrets">
In the Astral Orrery, you can often use cubes to block laser turrets. This is frequently the key to reaching breakable walls that hide a **Mina the Hollower Kear**.
</Callout>

</Section>
