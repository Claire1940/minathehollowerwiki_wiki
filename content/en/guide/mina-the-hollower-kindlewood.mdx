To begin your journey to Kindlewood, head back to the Western Wilds, specifically the area with the lava you traversed on your way to the last Generator. This time, instead of burrowing under unbreakable rocks, you'll be looking for breakable rocks along the top of the path.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Exploring the Overgrowth Region">
The Overgrowth is the initial area you'll encounter in Kindlewood. This lush, bushy zone is characterized by its vibrant greenery and peculiar yellow rocks. These rocks cannot be shattered by conventional attacks, requiring a different approach to clear your path.

<Callout type="warning" title="Environmental Hazards">
Some rocks in the Overgrowth are immune to regular attacks. Look for alternative methods, such as jumping from above or using fire, to clear these obstacles.
</Callout>

**Overgrowth Navigation & Puzzles:**

| Obstacle Type | Weakness | Method to Clear | Reward/Progression |
| :------------ | :------- | :-------------- | :----------------- |
| Yellow Rocks  | Impact   | Jump on top of them from above | Clears path to the left |
| Stubborn Rocks | Fire     | Use a torch to ignite them | Unlocks path to next area |
| Log Piles     | Attack   | Regular weapon attacks | Bones, hidden paths |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Break Yellow Rocks">
    Head south, jump over the gap, and tunnel north. Move under the brush to the left and pop out above the strange yellow rocks. Hit them from above to break them, clearing your way.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Chest & Find Residence">
    Enter the right tunnels to find a chest, then take the tunnel leading east to a residence.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ignite Stubborn Rocks">
    Inside the residence, locate the torch on the left. Use it to ignite the stubborn rocks, then burrow through the tunnel at the top right to advance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clear Fire-Spitting Enemy Path">
    Grab the torch from the bottom left, carry it over platforms to a rock on the right, and destroy it. Ascend the stairs. Bait the fire-spitting enemy to shoot a fireball at the rock on the left, clearing the next staircase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Create Shortcut & Prepare for Boss">
    Go up, then south, and knock down the shortcut rope. Take the path to the left and clear the area around the house before attempting to enter, as this triggers a boss fight.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Conquering the Madd House Boss">
The Madd House is a truly unique and challenging boss encounter. Upon attempting to enter the house, it springs to life, growing limbs and eyes, becoming a formidable foe. Understanding its movements and vulnerabilities is key to victory.

<Callout type="info" title="Madd House Weak Points">
The Madd House is primarily vulnerable when its legs are on the ground. Focus your attacks on its legs during these phases. Avoid being above the boss due to unpredictable hitboxes.
</Callout>

**Madd House Attack Patterns & Counter-Strategies:**

| Attack          | Description                                    | Counter-Strategy                                      |
| :-------------- | :--------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- |
| Ground Slam     | Smashes down, creating a shockwave.            | Burrow away or dash to the side before impact.        |
| Kick Attack     | Lunges forward with a kick.                    | Burrow to the opposite side to evade.                 |
| Flying Phase    | Lifts off the ground, making legs inaccessible. | Wait for it to land; jump attacks can hit if timed perfectly. |
| Bomb Drop (low HP) | Spawns bombs across the arena.                 | Steer clear of bombs until they explode.              |

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Offensive Strategy">
    - **Rush in and attack legs** when the boss is grounded.
    - Land 2-3 hits, then prepare to evade.
    - Use jump attacks during brief flying phases if confident.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Defensive Strategy">
    - **Prioritize burrowing** to avoid slams and kicks.
    - Stay below or to the sides of the boss.
    - Avoid getting above the boss to prevent unexpected damage.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

After defeating the Madd House, it will explode into Bones. Collect them, then enter the house. The tongue and eyeballs transform into shopkeepers, offering various goods, including a Kear. Consider acquiring the **Lightning Shield** trinket from the shop for future encounters.
</Section>
