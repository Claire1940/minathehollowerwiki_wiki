<Section icon={Target} title="How to Enter Mirrors and the Astral Orrery">

Entering a mirror is not intuitive for new players. If you attempt to use the standard interaction button, Mina will simply pose or look at her reflection. To actually transition into the Astral Orrery, you must use a specific physical movement technique.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Mirror">
    Find one of the six regional mirrors. Ensure the area is clear of enemies, as the transition takes a few seconds of uninterrupted movement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Phasing Run">
    Position Mina directly in front of the glass. Hold the forward direction (running into the mirror) without pressing any other buttons. Do not tap the interact key.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Astral Transition">
    After roughly two seconds of running against the glass, the screen will shimmer, and Mina will be transported to a color-coded room in the Astral Orrery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate the Mechanism">
    Once inside the Astral room, look for a **gold mechanism**. Striking this is mandatory; it unlocks the associated color blocks in the central hub and allows you to use that mirror as a fast-travel exit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Detailed Location Breakdown

1.  **Radiant Manor (Grand Hall):** This mirror serves as your introduction to the mechanic. Since it is located in the manor foyer, it is the easiest to return to when you need a quick exit from the Astral Orrery back to the center of the map.
2.  **Queensbury Crypt (Statue Head Hall):** This is a "Plundered Cache" area. You will see two statues missing their heads. While the heads are needed for a different puzzle, the mirror itself is hidden. Look for a flickering candle on the wall and burrow directly beneath it to enter the mirror chamber.
3.  **Bone Beach (Worm's Back):** Navigate through the skeletal remains of the beach until you find a campsite. One of the tents has a suspicious opening. Entering this tent leads directly to the mirror.
4.  **Coltrane Peak (Icebound Cavern):** This area is found several screens after the Rail Yard. The Icebound Cavern contains the Frozen Horror (a yellow ice beast). Once the beast is slain, go north, then immediately burrow through the right-hand wall.
5.  **Nox's Bayou (Moonlit Path):** This mirror is heavily guarded. You must climb the Moonlit Path and engage the Mock Moon. After the fight, the northern exit leads to a quiet room containing the green-coded mirror.
6.  **Septemburg (Crow Town):** Upon entering Crow Town, look for the farmhouse in the bottom-left corner. A lady stands on the balcony near a red chest. The door is initially locked; approach it to trigger a crow encounter, which forces the door open. The mirror is on the upper floor.

<Callout type="info" title="The Ossex House Mirror">
The mirror found in the house in Ossex cannot be entered from the overworld. It functions strictly as an **exit portal** from the Astral Orrery. Do not waste time trying to phase into it early in the game.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Astral Orrery Progression and Spark Generator">

The Astral Orrery is a complex puzzle hub where color-coded blocks prevent access to the northern exit. To reach the **Spark Generator**, you must strategically choose which mirrors to activate. While there are six mirrors in total, you only need to activate three to create a valid path through the shifting floors.

The central area, known as **Mirror's End**, features a glass floor where Mina can see the paths below. If you find yourself blocked by a blue wall, it means you haven't activated the Queensbury Crypt mirror mechanism.

<Callout type="success" title="Optimized Pathing">
For the fastest route to the Spark Generator, focus on the **Queensbury Crypt (Blue)**, **Nox's Bayou (Green)**, and **Septemburg (Orange)** mirrors. This combination clears a direct vertical path to the north.
</Callout>
