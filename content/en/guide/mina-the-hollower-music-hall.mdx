<Section icon={Map} title="Locating and Accessing the Music Hall">
The Music Hall is a prominent, yet initially inaccessible, location within Ossex, the main city in *Mina the Hollower*. Situated in the **Bowery** area, west of the City Center, it's guarded by a rather unwelcoming bouncer named Komodore who denies entry to Hollowers. Even attempting to burrow directly past him proves futile. However, there's a clever trick to bypass this obstacle and gain entry.

**Video Highlights:**
- Locate the NPC Tramp near the Music Hall.
- Pay Tramp 100 Bones for a key clue.
- Observe other NPCs entering the Music Hall.
- Burrow beneath a walking NPC to sneak past the guard.

To successfully enter, you'll need to use Mina's unique burrowing ability strategically. The key lies in becoming a "sneaky stowaway" by hiding beneath another townsperson as they walk past the guard.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tramp's Crucial Hint">
Near the Music Hall, you'll find a pauper NPC named **Tramp** (a raccoon at a campfire). Pay him **100 Bones**, and he'll reveal that Lionel often smuggled him into the Music Hall "between his toes." This seemingly cryptic message is your first clue to the entry method.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Entry into the Music Hall">
Gaining access to the Mina the Hollower Music Hall requires precise timing and execution of Mina's burrowing mechanic. Follow these steps carefully to sneak past the bouncer.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Music Hall and Guard">
    Head west from the City Center of Ossex into the Bowery. The Music Hall is a large building in the lower left corner, with Komodore, the bouncer, standing guard at the entrance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Observe Passing Townspeople">
    Stand a few paces away from the guard and watch the various townspeople as they walk in and out of the Music Hall. Identify an NPC that is about to veer towards the entrance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Burrow and Stowaway">
    As a townsperson approaches the bouncer, **burrow down** and position yourself directly underneath them. You need to move in lockstep with the NPC above ground.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Pass the Bouncer Unnoticed">
    Maintain your position under the NPC. The bouncer will be unable to stop you as you are effectively using the same path as the allowed townsperson. If timed correctly, you will pop out on the other side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Create a Shortcut">
    Once inside, interact with the nearby pipe to create a permanent shortcut. This allows you to re-enter the Music Hall without repeating the burrowing process, making future visits much easier.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Timing is Key">
If you burrow too early or too late, Komodore will shove you back, preventing entry. Don't worry, you can try as many times as needed until you perfect the timing. Practice makes perfect!
</Callout>

</Section>

### Recruiting the Frog Band

The Frog Band is found in the Backwaters, specifically in the large area with circular floating platforms, to the left of the Fishing Hole. They are missing their lead singer, whom you'll encounter after completing Nox's Bayou and repairing its tower.

**Frog Band Recruitment Process:**

| Step | Location | Task | Reward/Outcome |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **1: Find Band** | Backwaters (Nox's Bayou) | Locate three frogs near the northern edge. | Discover the missing singer quest. |
| **2: Find Singer** | Nox's Bayou (Waterfalls) | After tower repair, find singer behind a top-left waterfall. | Singer asks for help returning. |
| **3: Escort Singer** | Waterfalls to Backwaters | Burrow under the tiny singer, throw him across platforms. | Reunite band in Backwaters. |
| **4: Pay Fee** | Backwaters | Donate a small fee for transportation to the Music Hall. | Frog Band relocates to Music Hall. |

Once the Frog Band is reunited and moved to the Music Hall, the atmosphere becomes much livelier. Speaking to the Shark Man again will trigger a unique **mini-boss dance battle**. Defeating him awards you the **Pneumatic Armlet**.

<Callout type="info" title="Pneumatic Armlet">
The **Pneumatic Armlet** is a powerful Trinket that supercharges any item you throw at enemies, making them faster and stronger. It's an excellent reward for bringing music back to the Hall!
</Callout>
