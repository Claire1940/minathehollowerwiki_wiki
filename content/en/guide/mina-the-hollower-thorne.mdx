<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Mace">
    Before engaging Thorne, ensure Mina has the Mace equipped. Its ranged capabilities will allow you to poke Thorne safely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Burst Damage and Retreat">
    When attacking Thorne, do so in small, quick bursts. Inflict a few hits and then be ready to escape quickly if he initiates an attack. This hit-and-run tactic minimizes your exposure to damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Hollowing for Tornados">
    The Tornado Burst is best countered by using Mina's Hollowing ability. Burrow underground to become invulnerable to the expanding tornados. Practice the timing to pop up and continue the fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Healing">
    Keep an eye on your health bar. When healing, create a significant distance between Mina and Thorne. Healing too close will result in Thorne's attacks interrupting the process, wasting your healing items.
  </Step>
</Steps>
