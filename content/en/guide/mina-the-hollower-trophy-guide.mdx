### The Tenebrous Times & Mirrors
The newspaper is reactive. If you skip the recommended dungeon order, the paper will comment on your "unorthodox" approach. Checking the newsstands at each train station is a great way to track your progress. Additionally, keep a log of every Mirror you find. You need at least three for a major story gate, but finding them all is often linked to hidden collectibles.
