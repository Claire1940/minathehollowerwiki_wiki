<Section icon={Target} title="Progression and Character Growth">

Mina the Hollower utilizes a progression system known as "Boning Up." Bones act as the primary currency and experience points, similar to Runes in Elden Ring or Souls in Dark Souls. When you die, you drop your bones at the site of your demise. If you die again before retrieving them, those resources are lost forever.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Bones">
    Defeat enemies and find hidden treasures to accumulate Bones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find an Underlab">
    Locate these safe havens to spend your Bones on permanent stat increases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Trinkets">
    Find and swap trinkets to modify movement, defense, or attack power.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Sparks">
    Acquire Sparks to act as a safety net, preventing Bone loss upon death.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Underlabs serve as checkpoints that restore your health and flasks but also respawn all enemies in the world. This creates a tense gameplay loop where you must decide whether to push forward to the next lab or retreat to secure your current earnings.

| Stat/Upgrade | Effect | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attack Power** | Increases raw damage per hit | High |
| **Defense** | Reduces damage taken from hits | Medium |
| **Health Extension** | Adds permanent HP to your bar | High |
| **Trinket Slots** | Allows for more passive buffs | Essential |

<Callout type="success" title="The Proto Spark">
Prioritize obtaining the **Proto Spark** trinket. It resurrects you once per checkpoint visit, which is a literal lifesaver during the game's difficult 23-hour campaign.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Exploration and World Secrets">

The Tenebrous Isles are a non-linear open world. From the start, you can access four initial dungeons in any order. The game does not gate progress behind specific items; instead, your skill and your ability to find secret passages determine where you can go.

**Key Exploration Tools:**
- **Burrowing**: Cross gaps and enter small holes in the ground.
- **Iron Steed**: A bicycle sidearm used to jump massive chasms.
- **Trinket Combos**: Combine wall-grabs and increased burrow time to reach "unreachable" chests.
- **Screen Transitions**: Use them to reset enemy positions if you are overwhelmed during a runback.

| Region | Vibe/Theme | Key Challenge |

| **September** | Halloween/Gothic Horror | Managing lurking Resident Evil-style threats |
| **The Swamp** | Murky/Toxic | Environmental hazards and slow movement |
| **The Crypt** | Dark/Undead | Complex puzzles and trap-heavy corridors |
| **Main City** | Steampunk/Tech | NPC sidequests and high-end blacksmith shops |

<Callout type="info" title="Map Secrets">
Mina the Hollower does not provide a room-by-room map. You must rely on your visual observation. If a wall looks suspicious or a room has no exit, use your whip or sidearms to probe for breakable tiles.
</Callout>
