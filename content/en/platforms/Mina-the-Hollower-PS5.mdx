<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Hollowing">

Combat in **Mina the Hollower PS5** revolves around the Nightstar, a versatile whip that allows for precise directional attacks. Unlike many traditional 8-bit titles that limit movement during combat, Mina offers a high degree of mobility. The standout feature is the "Hollowing" mechanic, which allows Mina to burrow beneath the surface of the earth.

  <Card title="Hollowing Technique">

The strategic depth of Hollowing cannot be overstated. It is both a defensive tool and a puzzle-solving necessity. When burrowed, Mina is invincible to most surface-level hazards, allowing you to reposition during chaotic boss fights in Nox's Bayou or Ossex.

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Burrowing">
Don't just use Hollowing to dodge. Use it to close the distance between you and ranged bat enemies like Thorne's shock troopers to land a decisive whip strike.

The central conflict involves Mina's patron, Baron Lionel, and the traitorous bat, Thorne.

Restoring these generators is key to progressing the story and unlocking new regions.
