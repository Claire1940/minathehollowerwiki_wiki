<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Challenges: Progression and Customization">
**Mina the Hollower** is not for the faint of heart. Its difficulty is a clear homage to classic action RPGs, featuring over 20 diverse boss fights and intricately designed areas. However, the game provides ample tools for players to adapt and overcome. The "bone-up" system allows you to level up Mina's attack, defense, or sidearm abilities, or even crystallize bones to prevent loss upon death.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Explore Tenebrous Isle">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire and Upgrade Weapons">
    Choose from a variety of primary weapons like the fast Nightstar whip, the heavy-hitting Black Strike Wall, or the rapid Whisper and Vesper daggers. Each can be upgraded to unlock additional utility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Sidearms and Trinkets">
    Experiment with diverse sidearms for offensive projectiles or defensive options. Equip trinkets to modify Mina's playstyle, offering exotic effects that can turn the tide of battle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Level Up Mina's Abilities">
    Collect bones from defeated enemies. Use them to "bone up" and improve Mina's core stats (attack, defense) or sidearm power, or save them by crystallizing to prevent loss upon defeat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Adapt to Enemy Encounters">

### Accessibility and Modifiers

Yacht Club Games has thoughtfully included an exhaustive menu of quality-of-life and difficulty options. These modifiers, reminiscent of *Shovel Knight*'s cheat codes, can be enabled at any time to tweak the experience.

| Modifier Type | Examples                                          | Impact                                                              |
| :------------ | :------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Easier**    | Invincibility, super jump, floatier jumps, auto-jump, infinite burrow. | Reduces challenge, allows casual play, may disable achievements.     |
| **Harder**    | Increased enemy damage, reduced healing, fewer resources. | Significantly increases difficulty for veteran players seeking a challenge. |
| **QoL**       | Double walking speed, item suction, favoriting system. | Improves convenience and streamlines gameplay.                     |
