The "Hollowing" mechanic is the heart of the game's traversal. Unlike traditional platformers where jumping is the primary dodge, Mina relies on going beneath the threat. This adds a layer of depth to every encounter on Tenebrous Isle.

| Action | Xbox Input | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attack** | **X Button** | Swing the Nightstar whip in the facing direction. |
| **Hollow** | **A Button** | Burrow underground for a short duration. |
| **Sidearm** | **B Button** | Use the currently equipped secondary weapon. |
| **Inventory** | **View Button** | Swap Trinkets and check Spark Generator status. |
| **Dodge/Leap** | **Y Button** | Perform a quick hop to clear small obstacles. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Tip">
You can attack immediately after surfacing from a burrow. Use this to perform a "Pop-up" strike on unsuspecting foes for bonus damage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapons, Sidearms, and Trinkets">

To survive the Victorian Gothic horrors of Ossex and Nox's Bayou, you must curate a powerful loadout. Mina is a genius inventor, and her Spark Technology allows for a highly customizable playstyle. Whether you prefer a tanky build or a high-damage glass cannon, the Trinket system provides the necessary flexibility.

**Trinkets** are equippable items that provide permanent boosts or exotic effects. You can find these hidden in secret areas or purchase them from Baron Lionel’s associates.

| Trinket Name | Effect Type | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Spark Core** | Offensive | Increases whip damage by 15% at full health. |
| **Gothic Veil** | Defensive | Grants a brief shield after surfacing from a burrow. |
| **Clockwork Gear** | Utility | Reduces the energy cost of all Sidearms. |
| **Tenebrous Eye** | Exploration | Highlights destructible walls and hidden paths. |
| **Feral Fang** | Combat | Adds a bleed effect to whip strikes. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Management">
You can only equip a limited number of Trinkets at once. Leveling up Mina allows you to unlock more slots, so prioritize experience gain in the early game.
</Callout>

### Sidearm Arsenal
Sidearms offer tactical variety that the whip lacks. For instance, the **Dagger** is excellent for picking off flying bats like Thorne’s minions, while the **Hatchet** can hit enemies behind cover.

- **Dagger**: Fast, linear projectile with low damage.
- **Hatchet**: High-arcing throw that bypasses shields.
- **Volt Grenade**: Area-of-effect Spark damage for crowd control.
- **Iron Stake**: High knockback, useful for pushing enemies into pits.
