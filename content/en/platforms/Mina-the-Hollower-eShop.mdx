<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics and Combat System">
Mina the Hollower introduces a dynamic combat system that blends classic whip-based attacks with modern action RPG elements. As Mina, your primary weapon is the trusty whip, Nightstar, allowing you to lash at foes in true Castlevania style. However, the combat also draws heavily from Souls-like games, particularly Bloodborne, emphasizing fast-paced, brutal encounters that require precise dodging and strategic use of Mina's unique abilities.

A core mechanic is "hollowing" or "burrowing," which allows Mina to temporarily become invincible and reach otherwise inaccessible areas, akin to Metroidvania exploration. This ability is crucial for evading enemy attacks and navigating the intricate world. Defeated enemies drop "Sparks," similar to Blood Echoes, which can be used for equipment upgrades. Dying means losing these Sparks, requiring a return to the point of demise to retrieve them, adding a layer of challenge and risk.
