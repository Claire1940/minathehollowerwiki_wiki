<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics and Combat in Mina the Hollower">
**Mina the Hollower** thrusts players into a dark, action-packed world where mastering unique mechanics is key to survival. The game draws inspiration from classic titles, offering a challenging yet deeply satisfying combat system.

### Core Gameplay Elements

| Mechanic        | Description                                                                 | Strategic Use                                                     |
|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Hollowing**   | Burrow underground to evade hazards, monsters, and traverse quickly.        | Essential for dodging enemy attacks and accessing secret paths.    |
| **Whip (Nightstar)** | Mina's primary weapon, offering good range and swift attacks.               | Effective for keeping distance from foes and crowd control.       |
| **Sidearms**    | Special weapons like throwing knives, axes, or a dodge move.                | Consumes Jewels; crucial for burst damage or tactical retreats.   |
| **Trinkets**    | Modifiers found throughout the world that alter Mina's abilities.           | Customize your playstyle (e.g., item suction, increased defense). |
| **Bone Up/Leveling** | Collect bones from defeated enemies to improve stats (attack, defense, sidearms). | Invest wisely to overcome difficult bosses and areas.              |

<Callout type="warning" title="Challenging Combat">
Mina the Hollower features a four-directional combat system that can be initially tricky, requiring players to adapt to enemy patterns and positioning. Boss fights are multi-stage and demand precision.
</Callout>

### Mastering Combat Techniques

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand Four-Directional Combat">
    Combat in Mina the Hollower is restricted to four directions (up, down, left, right). This requires careful positioning and timing, as enemies can attack from various angles. Practice against weaker foes to get a feel for the hitboxes and your attack range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Hollowing for Evasion">
    Mina's unique "Hollowing" ability allows her to quickly move underground. This is not just for traversal but also a powerful defensive tool. Use it to dodge projectiles, bypass enemy charges, and reposition during intense boss encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Experiment with Sidearms and Trinkets">
    Don't stick to just one sidearm or trinket. Different situations call for different tools. The dodge sidearm, for instance, can be highly effective for invulnerability frames and repositioning, even restoring a small amount of health. Trinkets can significantly alter your gameplay, from drawing items towards you to boosting specific stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Your Health and Bones">
    Health recovery is Souls-like; damaging enemies fills a bar that allows you to heal. Bones are your currency for leveling up or can be crystallized to prevent loss upon death. Decide whether to level up immediately or save bones for tougher challenges.
  </Step>
</Steps>
