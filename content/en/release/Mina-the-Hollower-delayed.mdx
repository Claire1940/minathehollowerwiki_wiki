<Section icon={Target} title="The Development Odyssey: From Prototype to Pivotal Project">
**Mina the Hollower** began as a passion project by designer Alex Falner after the completion of *Shovel Knight* DLC in 2019. Initially envisioned as a smaller, quicker project compared to a *Shovel Knight* sequel, its scope grew significantly, leading to unforeseen challenges. The studio even split into two teams, one for *Mina* and another for a rumored 3D *Shovel Knight* project.

However, the rapid expansion of *Mina's* scope and internal bottlenecks, including Falner's slow design decisions, created significant hurdles. By 2024, a year after its initial projected launch, Yacht Club Games had to lay off staff, cut costs, and entirely pause the 3D *Shovel Knight* project to consolidate all efforts on *Mina*. Velasco took over lead, transforming the "less ambitious" title into their most significant undertaking to date.
