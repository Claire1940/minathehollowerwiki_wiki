<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics: The Hollowing">

The defining feature of the game is the **Hollowing** ability. This allows Mina to burrow underground, becoming temporarily invincible while moving at high speeds. This mechanic is integrated into every facet of the game, from dodging boss ultimate attacks to solving intricate environmental puzzles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Burrow">
    Press the dedicated Hollowing button to submerge Mina into the earth. While underground, you are immune to most standard projectiles and physical contact.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate Obstacles">
    Use burrowing to cross small gaps or move under low-hanging hazards. This is essential for traversing the poisonous swamps of Nox's Bayou.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Extended Leap">
    Rising from a burrow at the right moment allows Mina to perform an extended leap. This reach is necessary to find secrets hidden behind "textures that differ from the norm."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Combat Integration">
    Time your resurfacing to strike enemies from below or to reposition behind a boss during their recovery frames.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Resource Management">
Burrowing is powerful but makes you "vulnerable" in terms of visibility. You must carefully track your position beneath the soil to avoid resurfacing directly into a pit or an active hazard.
</Callout>

</Section>
