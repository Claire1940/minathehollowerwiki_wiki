<Section icon={Target} title="Development Journey and Delays">
The path to the **Mina the Hollower release time** has been anything but smooth. What started as a passion project by designer Alex Falner after the *Shovel Knight* DLC in 2019, quickly grew in scope. Initially envisioned as a smaller project, *Mina the Hollower* evolved into Yacht Club Games' biggest undertaking to date.

The development faced several hurdles:
- **Scope Creep**: The game's ambition grew significantly beyond its initial design.
- **Team Structure Challenges**: An attempt to split the studio into two teams (one for *Mina* and one for a *3D Shovel Knight* project) proved unsustainable.
- **Internal Restructuring**: By 2024, the two-team structure was abandoned, and all resources were consolidated back onto *Mina the Hollower*. This involved layoffs and pausing other projects.
- **Financial Pressure**: The studio burned through most of its capital, making the success of *Mina the Hollower* critical for its independence.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Success Factor">
