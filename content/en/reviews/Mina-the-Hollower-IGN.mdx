<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Mina's Unique Mechanics">
To truly excel in Mina the Hollower, understanding and utilizing its core mechanics is essential. From the versatile burrowing ability to strategic trinket usage and bone management, each element plays a critical role.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Burrow">
    Mina's burrowing ability is more than just a movement tool. It acts as a dash, a dodge, and an aerial leap. Practice using it to evade enemy attacks, cross gaps, and position yourself for optimal strikes. Effectively integrating burrowing into your combat flow makes Mina feel incredibly nimble.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Trinket Loadouts">
    Trinkets provide crucial buffs that can significantly alter your playstyle. Experiment with different combinations for exploration (e.g., long jumps, increased burrowing time) versus combat (e.g., DPS multipliers, revive, health extenders). Don't be afraid to swap them frequently at Underlabs to suit the immediate challenge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Bone Management">
    Bones are your primary experience points. Decide whether to "Bone Up" (spend them for permanent upgrades like attack or defense) at an Underlab or risk carrying them further for more gains. If you're struggling, grinding in easier areas to gain Bones can make tough encounters much more manageable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Embrace Aggressive Healing">
    The Plasma Vial healing system requires you to attack enemies to generate "Plasma" before you can fully heal. This encourages an offensive mindset even when low on health. Use Sidearms and Trinkets to maximize Plasma generation during critical moments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
