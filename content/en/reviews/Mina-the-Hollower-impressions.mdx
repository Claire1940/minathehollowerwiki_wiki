Beyond the main weapon, Mina can discover various sub-items and trinkets that enhance her offensive and defensive capabilities. <Callout type="warning" title="Strategic Sidearm Use">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Difficulty and Soulslike Elements">
While *Mina the Hollower* clearly draws inspiration from classic action-adventure titles, it also incorporates elements of the Soulslike genre, particularly in its difficulty and progression system. The combat and platforming are genuinely challenging, demanding swift reflexes and an understanding of the environment to prevail. Dying is a common occurrence, but the game ensures it's always fair and well-telegraphed, similar to *Shovel Knight*'s approach.

When Mina perishes, she drops an orb containing her collected bones (currency and experience) at the spot of her demise. Players then revive at the last visited Underlab and must retrieve their lost currency before dying again. This mechanic adds a layer of tension and risk-reward to every encounter, encouraging careful play and strategic banking of resources.

### Progression and Difficulty Adjustments

| Feature           | Description                                    | Impact on Gameplay                                   |
|-------------------|------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| **Bone Currency** | Used for upgrades and leveling up              | Adds risk/reward to exploration; lost on death       |
| **Underlab**      | Safe zone, health refill, item upgrades        | Acts as a "bonfire" for progression and recovery     |
| **Trinkets**      | Equipable items boosting stats                 | Customizable difficulty and playstyle enhancements   |
| **Leveling System**| Upgrade Attack, Defense, Tool Strength         | Direct impact on character power and survivability   |
| **Custom Options**| In-game toggles for easier/harder experience   | Allows players to tailor difficulty to their preference|

<Callout type="success" title="Customizable Challenge for All">
*Mina the Hollower* embraces a "friendly Soulslike" philosophy, offering fantastic game adjustment options that can be toggled on or off at any time, even mid-fight. This allows players to fine-tune the difficulty, making it accessible to those new to the genre while still providing a robust challenge for veterans.
</Callout>

The game empowers players to experiment with its systems, from weapon choices and trinket combinations to adjusting difficulty settings.
